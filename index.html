Welcome to Explore Rexburg!

The purpose of this site is to be a guide to people that have recently moved to or are visiting Rexburg. This site helps you discover local hidden gems and explore Rexburg smarter.

Looking for something new?

Fat Cats Rexburg is a family-friendly entertainment center with bowling, movies, arcade games, and delicious food. It’s perfect for date nights, group activities, or casual fun with friends.

Rexburg Rapids is the best way to cool off in the summer! Featuring water slides, a lazy river, splash zones, and more — it's a local favorite for families and students.
